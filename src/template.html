Glossary
Expiration:
The expiration date for the DAX and STOXX options is the 3rd Friday of each month
Days until expiration:
Days until the expiry date including weekends and public holidays
Delta
Change in the option price if the strike price changes by 1.
The delta is also used to estimate the probability of exercise:
Delta = 0.5 => 50% probability that the option can be exercised with a profit on the expiry date.
Future Delta Hedge
Hedge the strike prices in the future to be delta neutral
Gamma
Change in delta when the strike price changes by 1, accelerating the price change.
